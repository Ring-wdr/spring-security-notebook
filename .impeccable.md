## Design Context

### Users
Spring Security, JWT, filter chain, and role-gated frontend flows를 함께 학습하는 개발자와 수강자용 practice workspace다. 사용자는 강의나 문서를 따라가면서 로그인, 세션 확인, 보호된 라우트 탐색, 관리자 기능 검증을 한 화면 흐름 안에서 빠르게 오가야 한다.

### Brand Personality
The product should feel calm, disciplined, and cold. It should read like a controlled lab notebook rather than a playful demo or a glossy marketing page. The interface should project composure, precision, and technical seriousness without becoming harsh or visually noisy.

### Aesthetic Direction
Use a restrained light theme with cool-tinted neutrals, measured contrast, and editorial hierarchy. The visual language should feel closer to a security control dossier or a systems workbook than a card marketplace. The primary page structure should be a single composed surface with internal sections, dividers, rails, and compact tiles instead of stacked card-inside-card groupings.

### Design Principles
1. Preserve a single dominant surface per page and flatten interior hierarchy wherever possible.
2. Favor section rhythm, dividers, and alignment over repeated boxed containers.
3. Keep copy readable and left-aligned, with wide enough measures to avoid brittle wrapping.
4. Let the UI feel cool and technical through spacing, tone, and typography rather than flashy effects.
5. Make privileged states, route checks, and session context feel integrated into the learning flow, not isolated in side widgets.
